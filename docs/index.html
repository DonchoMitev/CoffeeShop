Coffee as a culture

Just coffee, good coffee or great coffee? If you choose the latter, you probably already know that over the past few years the way the world talked and thought about coffee has changed a lot. The driving force behind this change were not the big coffee chains, as would have guessed many, but independent small merchants for whom coffee is not just a drink but a culture in a cup that requires care and special attention at every stage until it falls into the hands of the client. That's exactly the philosophy of Coffee Shop.
